$$

# 2
$$
f(A) \supset f(B) \Rightarrow A \supset B
$$

$\iff$:f是单射

## 证明
$\implies$:
$$
\begin{gathered}
A=\{x\} \\
B=\{y\} \\
f(x)=f(y) \implies f(A) \supset f(B) \Rightarrow A \supset B \implies x = y
\end{gathered}
$$
$\impliedby$:
$$
f(A) \supset f(B) \Rightarrow A=f^{-1}(f(A)) \supset f^{-1}(f(B)) =B
$$


# 3

$$
f(A \cap B) \supset f(A) \cap f(B)
$$
$\iff$:f是单射

## 证明

$\implies$:

反证法，设
 $f(x)=f(y) \quad x \neq y$
令 $A=\{x\}, B=\{y\}$

$\impliedby$:
### 法1

$$y \in f(A) \cap f(B)$$

$$\Downarrow$$

$$\exists x_1 \in A, \exists x_2 \in B, f(x_1) = f(x_2) = y$$

$$\Downarrow (\text{因为 } f \text{ 是单射})$$

$$x_1 = x_2 = x$$

$$\Downarrow$$

$$y = f(x) \in f(A \cap B)$$

### 法2

1. 令 $C = f(A) \cap f(B)$
    
2. $\because C \subset f(A)$ 且 $C \subset f(B)$
    
3. $\therefore f^{-1}(C) \subset f^{-1}(f(A))$ 且 $f^{-1}(C) \subset f^{-1}(f(B))$
    
4. $\because f \text{ is injective} \implies f^{-1}(f(S)) = S$
    
5. $\therefore f^{-1}(C) \subset A$ 且 $f^{-1}(C) \subset B$
    
6. $\therefore f^{-1}(C) \subset A \cap B$
    
7. $\therefore f(f^{-1}(C)) \subset f(A \cap B)$
    
8. 由 $C \subset \text{Im}(f)$ 可得 $f(f^{-1}(C)) = C$
    
9. $\therefore C \subset f(A \cap B)$
    
10. 即：$f(A) \cap f(B) \subset f(A \cap B)$

### 证明
⇒：两边取 $f^{-1}$ 得

$$

f^{-1} f(A) \cap f^{-1} f\left(A^{c}\right)=\emptyset
$$
注意到$f^{-1} f(A) \supset A$   $f^{-1}f\left(A^{c}\right) \supset f\left(A^{c}\right)$
故$f^{-1} f(A)=A$

$\Leftarrow$:

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(A) & =f f^{-1}(V) \subset U \\
f\left(A^{c}\right) & =f f^{-1}\left(V^{c}\right) \subset V^{c}
\end{aligned}
$$

推论1：由 $\left[f^{-1}(v)\right]^{c}=f^{-1}\left(v^{c}\right)$

$A, B$ 饱和 $\Rightarrow A \cap B \quad A \cup B \quad A \backslash B=A \cap B^{\circ}$都饱和

$$
\begin{aligned}
